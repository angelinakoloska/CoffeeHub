Iced Chai Latte

An iced chai latte is a spiced tea drink with milk served over ice. This is a tea version of the traditional iced latte.

Ingredients

  • 1 chai tea bag
  • 1dl (1/2 cup) hot water
  • 1 tsp sugar or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1dl (1/2 cup) milk
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Add the tea bag, hot water, and sweetener of choice to a pitcher. Let the mixture sit for five minutes.

  2. Stir until the sweetener is gone. Then, remove the tea bag and place the tea in the fridge to chill for approximately five minutes.

  3. Add ice to a glass and add the chai tea concentrate. Finish it of with milk and stir gently. Serve and enjoy.
