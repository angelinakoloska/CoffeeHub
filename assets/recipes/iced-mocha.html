Iced Mocha

An iced mocha is a cold coffee drink that consists of a double shot of espresso, milk and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Double shot of espresso
  • 1 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 1/2 dl (1/4 cup) milk of choice
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Prep the espresso. Add chocolate syrup at the bottom of the glass and put the glass under the portafilter and pull a double shot of espresso.

  2. Stir the espresso, so you the chocolate will be mixed with the espresso.

  3. Froth the milk by using a french press or milk frother, or shake the milk by hand. You can also just pour straight milk.

  4. Top the espresso with the frothed milk and serve. You can also add chocolate sauce at the top.
