Iced Matcha Latte

An iced matcha latte is a green tea powder mixed with milk and poured over ice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 4dl (13 1/2oz) milk
  • 1/2 - 1tsp maple syrup (optional)
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Pour milk, matcha powder, and maple syrup into a tall glass. Stir by hand or use a milk frother until it is smooth and lump free. Always add the milk first, and the powder second, this is because so you donâ€™t have any powder at the bottom. (You can also add all the ingredients mentioned above in a blender, and blend until the mixture is smooth and lump free)

  2. Add ice to a glass. Pour the matcha mixture over the ice. Serve. Feel free to adjust.
