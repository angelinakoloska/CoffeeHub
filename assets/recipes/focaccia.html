Focaccia

Focaccia is an Italian flatbread where the dough is flavoured with olive oil and sometimes topped with herbs, vegetables, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg flour
  • 1 pack dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt in the dough
  • 9 dl luke warm water
  • 1 1/2 dl olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 garlic
  • Flake salt

Instructions

  1. Add flour, yeast, sugar, salt, chili powder and water into a bowl, and stir well together. Let the dough rest for about 40 minutes.

  2. Add olive oil into a little bowl. Peel the garlic and finely chop it or use a garlic press. Add thyme, rosemary, oregano, basil, salt and the garlic into the little bowl and stir.

  3. Find a baking tray and place on baking paper. Pour the dough into the baking tray and spread it. Dip your fingers into the olive oil mixture and make holes in the bread. Put some towels or use plast foil to cover the baking tray, and let it rest for another 40 minutes.

  4. Dip your fingers again and make holes, do not use up all of the mixture. Pour the rest of the oil mixture over the dough and spread it. Sprinkle flake salt. Set the oven to 200C degrees and bake for 15-25 minutes (depending on the oven) in the middle, until it has turned golden brown.
