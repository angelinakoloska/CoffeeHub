Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona coffee is a whipped iced coffee drink made with only three ingredients, and its made up of equal parts.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp instant coffee
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp hot water
  • Milk of choice
  • Ice cubes (optional)

How To

  1. Add instant coffee, sweetener of choice and hot water into a bowl and mix together, you can do it either by hand or with a milk skimmer. The mixture should be thick and frothy and reach a pale caramel color.

  2. Add the ice cubes in a glass, then pour over the milk . Then add the whipped coffee at the top. Serve

If you do not want to use sugar, you can replace it with other sweeteners, such as honey or maple syrup.
