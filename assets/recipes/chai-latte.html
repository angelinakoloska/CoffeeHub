Chai Latte

A chai latte is a spiced tea drink, which contain steamed milk and topped with foam. This is the tea version of a traditional latte, which is combined with espresso coffee and steamed milk.

Ingredients

  • Chai tea
  • 60 ml hot water
  • Steamed milk
  • Cinnamon powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. If you use a tea bag; Add tea bag to a cup. Boil some water and pour the boiling water over the tea. If you use chai powder; Add 3 tbsp or 34g of chai powder into a cup. Add hot water to the cup.

  2. Steam the milk similar to a latte and pour it to the cup. You can add cinnamon if you want. Serve.
