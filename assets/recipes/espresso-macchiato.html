Espresso Macchiato

An espresso macchiato is an espresso drink with a double shot of espresso topped with frothy steamed milk. Macchiato means “stained” or “marked”.

Ingredients

  • Double shot of espresso
  • 30ml (4oz) frothed milk

Instructions

  1. Grind coffee and pull a double shot of espresso. Check this guide on how to make an espresso.

  2. Pour 4 ounces of milk and froth the milk. You are supposed to only use about 1 ounce of the milk.

  3. Use a spoon to scoop or pour 2 tablespoons of milk into the espresso, and serve.
