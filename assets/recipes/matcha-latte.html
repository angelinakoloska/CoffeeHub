Matcha Latte

A matcha latte is a tea latte made with matcha green tea powder and steamed milk. The espresso in a traditional cafe latte is replaced with matcha, which gives it that green color and lightly bitter flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 - 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1/2dl (1/4) cup hot water
  • 1/2dl (1/4) cup warmed milk
  • Sweetener of choice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour hot water into a large mug, and then add the matcha powder and whisk until frothy. You can also use a milk frother.

  2. Add the milk and continue to whisk until well combined

  3. Feel free to add less or more of the ingredients and enjoy.
