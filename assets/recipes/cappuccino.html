Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink with steamed milk foam and sometimes flavoured with cinnamon or chocolate powder.

Ingredients

  • One or two shots of espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Sweetener (optional)
  • Cinnamon powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pull a shot of espresso. (Click here to see how to make an espresso ).

  2. If you have an espresso machine, steam the milk to 65 degrees celsius (or 150 fahrenheit). If you do not have an espresso machine, you can gain the same result by using a milk frother or a french press, just heat up the milk first.
  3. Circle the espresso slowly. Tilt the cup, and pour from the side of the jug, and not the spout. Pouring from the side of the jug will result a much foamier cappuccino.
