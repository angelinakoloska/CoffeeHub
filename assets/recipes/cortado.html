Cortado

Cortado is a spanish coffee drink. The cortado is served differently depending on where you are in the world. Cortado is made with equal parts milk and espresso. A traditional cortado would be of 1:1 ratio.

Ingredients

  • A double shot of espresso (40 ml)
  • 40ml steamed milk

Instructions

  1. Prep the espresso and pull a two shots of the espresso. It should brew between 20 to 30 seconds and have a nice crema on top.

  2. Steam the milk to lightly textured milk. The temperature should be around 55-65 degrees celsius (130-150 degrees Fahrenheit).

  3. Pour the milk into the espresso and serve. If you want to make an iced cortado simply use the same 1:1 espresso to milk ratio, but skip the steaming and pour over ice.
