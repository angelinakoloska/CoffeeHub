Mocha

An americano is an espresso drink made with hot water and espresso. It can be made with a single or a double shot of espresso, and varying ratios of water. The water for an americano can be customized.

Ingredients

  • Espresso
  • Hot water

Instructions

  1. Pull and prep a single or a double shot of espresso (personal preference) directly into a cup.

  2. Boil the water by using a regular or electric teapot.

  3. Pour 2/3 of the boiling water into the espresso and serve.
