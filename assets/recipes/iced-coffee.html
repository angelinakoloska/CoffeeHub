Iced Coffee

Iced coffee is a coffee drink served cold. It can either be prepared by brewing it normally and then serve it over ice or in cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tbsp instant coffee (You can add less or more)
  • 1-2 dl (1/2 - 1 cups) hot water
  • 1 1/2 dl (2/3 cups) Milk or creamer (optional)
  • Ice
  • Sweetener (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew your coffee how you would do normally.

  2. Let the brewed coffee sit out until it is cooled to room temperature.

  3. Find a tall glass, add ice and pour the cooled coffee over it, and serve.
